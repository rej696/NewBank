This document details the protocol for interacting with the NewBank server.  

A customer enters the command below and sees the messages returned 

SHOWMYACCOUNTS
Returns a list of all the customers accounts along with their current balance 
e.g. Main: 1000.0 

NEWACCOUNT <Name>
e.g. NEWACCOUNT Savings
Returns SUCCESS or FAIL

MOVE <Amount> <From> <To>
e.g. MOVE 100 Main Savings 
Returns SUCCESS or FAIL

PAY <Person/Company> <Ammount>
e.g. PAY John 100
Returns SUCCESS or FAIL

// Micro-loan service 
OFFERLOAN <Amount> <FromAccountX> <ToPersonY> <Interest>
e.g. OFFERLOAN 500 Main John 0.05
Returns SUCCESS or FAIL

SHOWMYOFFEREDLOANS <Loan Number> <Account Number> <Amount> <Interest Rate>
e.g. SHOWMYOFFEREDLOANS
Returns: Success. Loan of 500.0 offered from 62345678 for 365 days with interest of 5%

SHOWOPENLOANS <Loan Number> <Amount> <Term> <Interest Rate>
e.g. SHOWOPENLOANS
Returns: Success. Loan of 500.0 offered from 82345678 for 365 days with interest of 5%

ACCEPTLOAN <Loan Number> <Account>
e.g. ACCEPTLOAN 1 11111111
Returns: Success. Loan number 1 accepted by account 11111111.
